About ZombieTweet

ZombieTweet is like a video recorder for Twitter.

If you can't follow tweets about your preferred TV show during the episode or if you host a conference you can schedule a recording.

When you are ready to watch the episode or analyze discussions about the conference you can reload the tweets like press "play".
